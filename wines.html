The modern Ukrainian wine market is flourishing, with more and more wineries focusing on quality and the unique character of their wines. MAIN WINE REGIONS

Black sea

In the Black Sea region, the sun, sea breeze, and fertile soils create a terroir unlike any other. From the steppes of Bessarabia to the limestone-kissed coast, every vineyard tells a story of resilience and flavor.

Zakarpattia

In Zakarpattia, vineyards bask in Carpathian sunlight, sheltered from northern winds. Once known for whites, the region now ripens reds too, growing on sunlit hillside slopes with a unique mountain microclimate.
